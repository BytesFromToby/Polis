**Traits:**

- Ambitious (strong)
- Pragmatic (moderate)
- Distrustful of outsiders (moderate)

**Relational traits:**

- Tolerates The Craftsmen's League (useful but beneath them)

**Disposition toward the Mayor:**
- Neutral — respects authority but not loyalty. Will cooperate if it serves them.

**Personality notes:**
- The Ironmongers do not ask permission. They move, and expect others to adjust.
- Vorn Ashpike keeps his word once given, but gives it rarely.
- They measure every interaction in terms of what it costs and what it returns.
- They have little patience for weakness, in themselves or others.
