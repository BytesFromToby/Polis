Relationship Traits:
  Friend   — naturally assist, support, ally with
  Foe      — naturally block, attack, compete with
  Hide     — avoid attention from, obscure actions against
  Client   — want something from, seek to use
  Neutral  — no natural pull either way

Mechanical effect on NPC behavior:
  Friend:  Assist, Support, Recruit actions weighted toward this domain
  Foe:     Attack, Block, Steal weighted toward this domain
  Hide:    Obscure weighted when this domain active. Avoid drawing attention.
  Client:  Spy, Assist, Recruit toward this domain. Seeking something specific.
  Neutral: No weight modification.
