SETTING
================================================================================

Rivers Point is a river port city where three rivers converge to form
a navigable waterway downstream. Upstream are waterfalls and crags leading
into the Ironside Mountains. The mountains are mined and ore is brought
to Rivers Point for transport and processing.

The city sits at a natural chokepoint — the only viable route between
the mountain mines and the downstream markets. Many roads converge here
because the Groaning River downstream is too wide for road crossings.

Commerce is the lifeblood of Rivers Point. The Mayor and Noble Houses
who run the city must keep the docks moving and the guilds happy. In a
city where everything that moves sells, smuggling and crime are common.
But general peace must be maintained — when commerce stops, no one is happy.


================================================================================
DOMAINS
================================================================================

GUILDS 
  Merchants, craftsmen, brokers, commercial law.
  Economic authority of the city. Control pricing,
  contracts, and the legitimacy of trade.

DOCKS
  Transport, longshoremen, river pilots, warehouses.
  Physical control of movement. The heart of the city.
  Everything passes through the Docks.

NOBLE HOUSES
  Landed gentry, mine owners, political authority.
  Hold the Mayoral seat and mining rights upriver.
  Old money and political power combined.

CITY WATCH
  Enforcement, order, corruption.
  Street and harbor enforcement. Answer to whoever
  pays them most. Keep the peace — or don't.

UNDERWORLD
  Thieves guild, smugglers, fences.
  Parasitic on the ore trade. Embedded throughout
  the city. Skimming from every transaction.

TEMPLE
  Religion, healing, moral authority.
  Minister to laborers and sailors. Neutral ground
  in disputes. The Healing House serves everyone.

COMMONS
  Dockworkers, caravan hands, laborers.
  The fuel that runs Rivers Point. Exploited by most,
  protected by few. High numbers, low individual power.

ARCANE
  Mage guild, assayers, magical services.
  Ore quality testing, weights and measures,
  commercial magic. Serve whoever needs them.

REGISTRY
  Records, charters, licenses, port authority.
  Issues trading charters, records cargo manifests,
  assigns warehouse rights, settles ownership disputes.
  Small but indispensable — every transaction needs a seal.


================================================================================
FACTIONS
================================================================================

--- GUILDS ---

The Ironmongers
  Ore processing, smelting, metalwork.
  Oldest and most powerful guild.

The Traders Compact
  Brokers, merchants, import/export.

The Craftsmens League
  Secondary crafts, goods production.


--- DOCKS ---

The Stevedores Union
  Longshoremen, loading, labor muscle.

River Pilots Guild
  Navigation, barge captains, river knowledge.

The Warehouse Masters
  Storage, logistics, inventory control.


--- NOBLE HOUSES ---

House Valdris
  Owns primary mountain mining rights.
  Effectively controls ore supply.

House Merren
  Old money. Controls prime warehouse district real estate.

House Calder
  Runs the Mayoral seat currently. Political authority.


--- CITY WATCH ---

The Watch
  Street level enforcement.

The Harbor Guard
  Docks and river enforcement.
  Separate chain of command from The Watch.


--- UNDERWORLD ---

The Dockside Brotherhood
  Smuggling, protection rackets.
  Embedded in Docks labor.

The Fences
  Stolen goods, black market, ore skimming operation.


--- TEMPLE ---

Temple of the Flowing Passage
  Primary temple. River deity.
  Ministers to sailors and laborers.

The Healing House
  Medical, hospice, herb work.
  Neutral ground in disputes.


--- COMMONS ---

The Caravan Workers
  Muleteers, drovers, road laborers.

The River Laborers
  Day workers, unorganized dockworkers.
  Distinct from the Stevedores Union.


--- ARCANE ---

The Assayers Circle
  Ore quality testing, weights and measures,
  commercial magic.

The Wayfarers Lodge
  Magical services for travelers.
  Maps, weather reading, message sending.


--- REGISTRY ---

The Hall of Records
  Deeds, charters, ownership documents.

The Port Manifest Office
  Cargo inspection, trade licenses, berth assignments.
